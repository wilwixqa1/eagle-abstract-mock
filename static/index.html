Since 1979

A family firm, built on clients' success.

Eagle Abstract was founded in 1979 by William F. Wendt, a practicing real-estate attorney who believed title work should come with responsive, personalized service. Today, co-owners Billy and Barbara Wendt — with the firm since 1983 — lead an in-house team of title specialists and counsel carrying that same standard forward.

As an authorized agent for Fidelity National Title and Westcor Land Title, we handle everything from courtesy recordings to the most complex title challenges in New York real estate.
