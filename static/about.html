About

Forty-seven years of clear title.

Eagle Abstract is what a title agency looks like when the same family answers the phone for five decades.

Eagle Abstract was founded in 1979 by William F. Wendt, a practicing real-estate attorney who saw the title process from his clients' side of the table — and believed it should come with a more comprehensive understanding of title insurance and genuinely responsive, personalized service.

Today, co-owners Billy Wendt and Barbara Wendt — each with the firm since 1983 — lead an in-house team of title specialists and counsel who carry that same standard forward. The conviction hasn't changed in five decades: our success depends on our clients' success.

As an authorized agent for Fidelity National Title Insurance Company and Westcor Land Title Insurance Company, and a member of the New York State Land Title Association, we handle everything from courtesy deed recordings to the most complex commercial title challenges in New York — with the goal, on every file, of getting the transaction to closing with every potential problem handled in advance.
